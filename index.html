What we do

Forestry Operations & Licensing

Forestry operations aimed at converting trees into products usable by the industrial process. Licensing process for logging and/or plantation.

Cleaning, Thinning, and planting

Pruning, cutting, and thinning of various types of trees and shrubs. We provide a wide range of forest cleaning services, utilizing specialized equipment for clearing operations. We also cultivate land and undertake the planting of all types of trees.

Felling & Loading

Cutting down trees using appropriate machinery and equipment. Loading involves the transport operation of moving timber from the felling site to the yard.

Buying & selling

We supply wood for pulp, sawmills, and biomass for thermal power plants, and purchase raw wood for subsequent cutting, loading, transportation, and supply to our customers.
